On the Anderson sewer extension: The engineering is done, stamped, and Town-approved — that's the expensive and time-consuming part. The physical installation is a buyer/builder cost. The engineer's opinion of probable cost (JVA, Oct 2021) was $135,100. A contractor bid from Randy Kneebone Excavating (June 2021) came in at ~$130,000. Both sets of plans transfer with the property. If buying both Anderson lots together, the extension serves both from a single pull.
Town of Erie tap fees (2025 rates — 3/4" tap, typical single-family):
Water Tap: $17,290  ·  Wastewater Tap: $8,860  ·  Water Dedication Fee: $31,400
Combined: $57,550 for a standard single-family connection. These are one-time fees paid at permit time. See the full fee schedule (all tap sizes) at erieco.gov → Fees & Rates.
At 275 Holbrook and 270 Holbrook, water and sewer taps are already paid and in place — this cost is behind you on those two properties.
